About Us

MES is a comprehensive solution that integrates Quality Management System (QMS) with Manufacturing Execution System (MES) to provide complete visibility and control over your manufacturing processes. Our system monitors, documents, analyzes, controls, and manages the entire manufacturing workflow.

MES focuses on three key pillars of manufacturing success: OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness), Profitability, and Sustainability. By implementing our solution, you can achieve higher efficiency, reduce waste, and maintain consistent quality standards.

Key Modules & Features

Distributed Control System (DCS)

Go beyond traditional SCADA with our advanced DCS that provides real-time monitoring and control of your manufacturing processes.

Schedule & Plan

Optimize your production scheduling for maximum OEE and efficiency with our intelligent planning module.

Process & Consumption

Understand the complex relationships between process parameters, settings, and material consumption to reduce waste.

Production Tracking

Maintain complete historical data on part tracking to ensure accountability and transparency throughout your production process.

Quality & Lab Integration

Correlate quality and lab data with production metrics to generate actionable KPIs and maintain consistent quality standards.

ETP & Waste Management

Monitor your water balance and waste streams to ensure environmental compliance and sustainability.
